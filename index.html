Frequently Asked Questions

Fuki AI uses advanced typography-aware models that achieve 95%+ text accuracy. Our AI understands letter spacing, font weights, and text placement to ensure crisp, readable text in every generated image.

We support PNG and JPEG formats with resolutions from 512x512 up to 8K (8192x8192) depending on your plan. All images are generated at 300 DPI for high-quality printing.

Pro and Studio plan users have full commercial rights to their generated images. Free plan users can use images for personal projects only. All images are royalty-free with no attribution required.
